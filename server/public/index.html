Tales of Casteria

I was bored and thought of a small fantasy story with characters, and thought I could display them here

See characters

Max the Hero

Class Warrior

Backstory
Coming from the villages of Al-Hambra, he has set out on a journey for revenge against the empire.

Shara the Ranger

Class Ranger

Backstory
An Elven Girl who had her tribe's village forcefully raized during the empires sieges. She has joined Max on his quest

Marden the Dwarf

Class Support

Backstory
Descending from the Mountains of The Forge, deep inside the capital of Argana, after the Empire's unjust policies led to the collapse of the Dwarven league, Marden has joined Max on his quest to save the Dwarven Alliance, keeping the previously warring tribes in harmony.

Casitra the wise

Class Wizard

Backstory
After waging a Holy war on all Demon-kin, witnessing the purge of all Demon Territory after the spread of the new religion taking the continent over en masse from the Empire. Casitra joins Max to save her people.

Sparrow the Root

Class Tank

Backstory
After rapid Industrialization has led to the acceleration of pollutants on Gaia, the Tree of Life, source of energy for all living plants has weakened over time, leading to the weakened life force of all Plantkin, Sparrow in an attempt to reverse all damage to his kin has join forces with Max.
